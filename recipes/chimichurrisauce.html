Chimichurri Sauce

Description

Chimichurri is a very popular sauce for grilled meats originating in Argentina and Uruguay, it can also be used as a marinade. This sauce is a bright zingy addition to any asado, and has many varitations, including a red chimichurri. The recipe below is a more traditional Argentine version.

Ingredients

  • Fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • Minced garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Red (or white) wine vinegar
  • Red chili or dried chili flakes
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except for the extra virgin olive oil to the bowl of a food processor. Process until finely minced. Careful not to over-process or over-blend.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a medium bowl and gently mix in olive oil. Allow the sauce to rest for 15 minutes before seasoning with additional salt, to taste. Best served at room temperature.
